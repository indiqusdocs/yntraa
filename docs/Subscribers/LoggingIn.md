# Logging In

To log in to your Yntraa Cloud, follow these steps:
1. Navigate to [https://account.yotta.com](https://account.yotta.com). The following screen appears :
   ![login](img/login.png)
2. Enter your **Email** and **Password**.
3. (Optional) Select the **Remember me** checkbox to stay signed in on your current device.
4. Click **Sign In**.
