# Logging In

To access the Yntraa cloud platform, you must log in with your registered email and password.

To log in to your Yntraa cloud platform, follow these steps:

1. Navigate to [https://account.yotta.com/](https://account.yotta.com/). The following screen appears :
   
   ![login1](img/login1.png)
   
2. Enter your **Email** and **Password**.
3. (Optional) Select the Remember me checkbox to stay signed in on this device
4. Click **Sign In**.
