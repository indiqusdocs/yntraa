# Signing Up

To get started with the Yntraa Cloud, you must create an account on the One Yotta account. 

1. To create a new One Yotta account and access all services, navigate to [https://account.yotta.com](https://account.yotta.com). The following screen appears:
   ![sigin](img/sigin.png)
   
2. Click on the **Sign Up**. The following screen appears:
   ![signuppage](img/signuppage.png)

3. On the **Sign Up** screen, provide the following details:
   
    - **First Name** and **Last Name**.
    - **Email Address**.
    - **Country Code** and **Mobile Number**.
    - **Company** or **Individual** as your account type.
    - **Company Name**
    - **Country**
    - **State**
    - **City**
    - **Postal Code**

4. Click **Submit**. The following screen appears:   ![signup3](img/signup3.png)

5. Follow the instructions in your registered email and click on **START NOW** button to activate your account.    ![signup4](img/signup4.png)
