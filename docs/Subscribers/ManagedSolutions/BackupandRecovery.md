# Backup and Recovery

Backup and Recovery is a managed service that protects critical business data by creating secure backups and enabling fast restoration. It provides resilience against data loss, cyber threats, and system failures by maintaining reliable recovery points and reducing downtime.

To activate the desired Backup and Recovery service, perform the following steps:
1. Navigate to **Other Services** > **Backup and Recovery**. ![br](img/br.png)
2. Click the **Activate** button. ![bractivation.png](img/bractivation.png)
3. Select the I have read and agreed to the **End User License Agreement** and **Privacy Policy** option, and click **Confirm Activation** button.
   
Once submitted, a support ticket will be automatically generated for the operations team for further processing.
   
For more information about the Multi-Tenant Backup, Recovery, and Archival service, [click here](downloads/BRaaS.pdf).
