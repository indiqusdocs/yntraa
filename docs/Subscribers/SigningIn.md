# Signing In

To **Sign In** to your Yntraa Cloud, follow these steps:
1. Navigate to Yntraa Cloud [url](https://portal.yntraacloud.ai/). The following screen appears :
   ![sigin](img/sigin.png)
2. Enter your **Email** and **Password**.
3. (Optional) Select the **Remember me** option to stay sign in on your current device.
4. Click the **Sign In** button.
