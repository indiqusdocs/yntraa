# Managing Quick Plan Categories
Quick Plan categories allow to group Quick Plans visually and semantically under the Marketplace section on the Yntraa cloud platform.

To set up and manage Yntraa Quick Plan categories, navigate to **Settings > Quick Plans**.
![Managing QuickPlan Categories](img/ManagingQuickPlanCategories.png)

:::note
Once created, a Quick Plan category can not be deleted.
:::
