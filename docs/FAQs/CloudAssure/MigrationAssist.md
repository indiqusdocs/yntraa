# Migration Assist


<details><summary>**What is Cloud Migration Assist?**</summary>Cloud Migration Assist is a structured service that supports all types of migrations, including on-premises to cloud, cloud-to-cloud, and legacy environment migrations. It ensures secure and efficient movement of workloads, applications, databases, and data with minimal disruption and a controlled transition.</details>

<details><summary>**How does Migration Assist fit within the Cloud Assure portfolio?**</summary>Migration Assist is the second pillar of Cloud Assure, focused on executing migrations securely and efficiently after assessment and planning are completed.</details>

<details><summary>**What migration approaches are supported?**</summary>We support rehost (lift-and-shift), re-platform, refactor, repurchase, retain, and retire strategies, aligned with business goals.</details>

<details><summary>**Which workloads can be migrated?**</summary>Depending on the mutually agreed scope, we can migrate applications, databases, infrastructure components, platforms, and even entire data centres.</details>

<details><summary>**Do you support cloud-to-cloud migrations?**</summary>Yes, we support migrations between public (Yntraa Cloud, AWS, AZURE), private, hybrid, and multi-cloud environments, including AWS, Azure, and Yntraa Cloud.</details>

<details><summary>**What is the difference between Lift-and-Shift and Re-platforming?**</summary>Lift-and-shift moves workloads with minimal change, while re-platforming upgrades certain components to improve performance and compliance without changing core architecture.</details>

<details><summary>**Can you handle database migrations across different engines?**</summary>Yes, both homogeneous (for example, SQL to SQL) and heterogeneous (for example, SQL Server to PostgreSQL) migrations are supported.</details>

<details><summary>**Does the service support both online and offline data migrations?**</summary>Offline (using customer or provider devices) and online (direct transfer or with Data Mover tools) methods are available depending on bandwidth, data size, and security needs.</details>

<details><summary>**Do you provide data centre migration services?**</summary>Yes, including physical (lift and shift), swing move (zero downtime), and logical (virtual/cloud-based) migrations.</details>

<details><summary>**What stages are followed in a migration project?**</summary>Assessment, planning, scheduling, execution (rehost/refactor), validation testing, and final cutover.</details>

<details><summary>**What is the customer responsible for?**</summary>Providing access, approvals, and credentials, participating in testing and validation, and ensuring backups of critical data before migration.</details>

<details><summary>**What is the service provider responsible for?**</summary>Strategy execution, tooling, migration execution, security baselines, validation, and knowledge transfer.</details>

<details><summary>**What is the Migration Factory model?**</summary>It is a scalable framework with standardized processes, governance, and automation to execute migrations at scale with minimal disruption.</details>

<details><summary>**Will there be downtime during migration?**</summary>Some downtime may occur during the cutover phase; however, downtime windows are planned in advance and agreed upon with business stakeholders. Where required, low impact or near-zero downtime migration approaches can be designed and executed in collaboration with a specialized team. Techniques such as swing migrations or phased cutovers are applied where technically feasible to minimize business disruption.</details>

<details><summary>**How is data integrity ensured during migration?**</summary>Through encryption, replication, validation checks, and rollback mechanisms.</details>

<details><summary>**What risks are associated with migration?**</summary>Technical (tool incompatibility), operational (downtime), resource availability, third-party dependencies, and scope creep. Each is addressed through mitigation planning.</details>

<details><summary>**Does Migration Assist support compliance requirements?**</summary>Yes, migration is aligned with governance, security baselines, and compliance standards like ISO 27001, GDPR, or DPDP, if specified in the SoW.</details>

<details><summary>**Are tools and licenses included in the service?**</summary>No, third-party tools, OEM licenses, or cloud-native utilities are billed separately unless explicitly included in the SOW.</details>

<details><summary>**How is pricing determined?**</summary>Pricing is effort-based and varies based on the migration type, complexity, and scope. Data migration services, such as bulk data transfers priced per GB or TB, are charged separately based on data volume.</details>

<details><summary>**Do you provide your own transfer devices?**</summary>Yes, for offline data movement, we offer secure provider devices (like AWS Snowball, Azure Data Box, or Yntraa Cloud appliances).</details>

<details><summary>**What happens after migration is completed?**</summary>A formal handover takes place with final validation, documentation delivery, and knowledge transfer sessions.</details>

<details><summary>**Do you provide managed services after migration?**</summary>Yes, if included under the support model. Otherwise, operations are handed back to the 
customer team.</details>

<details><summary>**What optimisation activities are included post-migration?**</summary>Right-sizing, performance tuning, enabling automation, CI/CD, and cost optimization as applicable.</details>

<details><summary>**Can Migration Assist modernize workloads during migration?**</summary>Yes, we can re-platform, containerize, or transition to PaaS solutions depending on 
workload readiness and business goals.</details>

<details><summary>**Why should I choose Migration Assist?**</summary>Because it ensures a secure, phased, low-risk migration with minimal disruption, embedded compliance, and expert-led execution tailored to your environment.</details>
