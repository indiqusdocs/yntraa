# Signing Up

To get started with the Yntraa cloud platform, new users must create an account through a straightforward sign-up process. Whether you're accessing the platform for the first time or looking to register for cloud services, the platform offers a user-friendly interface designed to make account creation simple and secure. 

Signing up on the One Yotta platform is a simple process designed to help new users get started quickly. Whether you're accessing cloud services or other features, creating an account is your first step. Just click the **Sign Up** link on the login page and follow the guided instructions to register and begin using the platform. To create a new One Yotta account and gain access to all services, follow these steps:

1. Navigate to the official One Yotta signup page via [https://account.yotta.com/](https://account.yotta.com/)
2.  In the login box, click **Not a member? Sign Up**.
3. Enter your **First Name** and **Last Name**.
4. Type your **Email Address**.
5. Select your **Country Code** and enter your **Mobile Number**.
6. Choose **Company** or **Individual** as your account type.
7. Select the checkbox to agree to the **Terms & Privacy Policy**.
8. Click **Submit** to complete registration.
9. Follow the email or mobile verification steps to activate your account.
## Completing Profile Details

After registering One Yotta account, you will be prompted to complete profile for verification. Follow these steps:

1. Verify or update your **Name** (may be pre-filled).
2. If applicable, check **PAN Applicable** and enter your **PAN Number**.
3. Fill in **Address Line 1** (mandatory) and **Address Line 2** (optional).
4. Select **Country**, **State**, and enter **City** and **Postal Code**.
5. If applicable, check **GST Applicable** and enter your **GSTIN**.
6. Choose **Taxation Type**:
    - Indian Organization or Individual (Non-SEZ)
    - Indian Organization or Individual (Located in SEZ)
7. Click **Submit** to complete verification.
