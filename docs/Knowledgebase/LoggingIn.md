# Logging In
To access the Yntraa cloud platform and its features, users must log in using their registered email and password. Follow these steps to login securely.

1. Navigate to [https://account.yotta.com/](https://account.yotta.com/).
2. Enter your **Registered Email**.
3. Enter your **Password** (click the eye icon to view it if needed).
4. (Optional) Check **Remember me** to stay signed in on this device.
5. Click **Sign In** to access your account.
