# Version 1.0.0.0
```
Date: 09.02.2026
Type: Major
```

## About this Release
Yntraa Cloud, powered by the Apiculus Solution Stack, introduces a self-service cloud user experience delivered from Yotta NM1. The platform enables native consumption of essential Compute, Network and Storage services, and extends it to the offline delivery of Yotta’s entire portfolio of Cloud, Network, Cybersecurity and Managed Service portfolio. The Yntraa Cloud platform integrates with Yotta’s key BSS including One Yotta, SFDC, Service Now and SAP for business process automation.

This version of Yntraa Cloud introduces the following capabilities for subscriber (customer) and Yotta (SP) admin users.
- Subscriber account signup, onboarding and login via One Yotta.
- Team/child user management and voucher redemption.
- Logged-in redirection for ticketing on Service Now.
- Billing and invoicing via programmatic usage exchange with SAP.
- High-level service consumption dashboard.
- Complete life cycle for Native services.
- Online order management for Non-native services via Quick Plans.
- Extensive reports and local logins for Yotta service admins.

## Known Issues
We are currently encountering challenges in managing SCF flows across all services. This is primarily due to ongoing issues with billing and contract dates being addressed by the SFDC Support Team. Once we receive the correct information from their end, we will proceed with our implementation.

- The team is actively mapping Windows and RHEL instances to their correct service IDs. Once this mapping is complete, these Virtual Machines will be ready for creation and provisioning.
- Work is currently in progress on the AutoScale feature.
- Following the receipt of inter-dependent SKU validations from the BA Team last week, we are now in the process of implementing these validations within our system.
- The Engineering team is finalizing the mapping activities for Quick Plans. We anticipate being able to raise requests for Quick Plans directly via the Yntraa Subscriber portal by February 12th, 2026.
